Resources for language study
This website containes three programs I developed that proved useful to my own language studies.
The ParallelTextApp enables you to read through a text and a corresponding parallel translation. While reading you have access to a self chosen dictionary and, if applicable, a transliteration of the original.
As a final result after processing your complete text you can produce a HTML-file that combines the original text and its translation where all words in both texts are hyperlinks to selected dictionaries.
As an example of this whole procedure I give the text Selbstdenken by Arthur Schopenhauer. The original version I found here and its corresponding translation I found here. There a numerous examples of books, novels, song texts and the like and there translated counterparts scattered over the internet.
Having only a limited knowledge of the original language concerned, reading it accompanied by its translated counterpart can be worthwhile.
The AddDictionaryApp changes all words in a plain text into hyperlinks to a selected dictionary.
This saves you the tedious job of looking up words in a dictionary.
As an example of this I again give the text Selbstdenken by Arthur Schopenhauer in this version.
Finally the RehearsalApp is a word rehearsal tool.
You can also use it to rehearse words using the Greek, Cyrillic, Hebrew or Arabic alphabets.
